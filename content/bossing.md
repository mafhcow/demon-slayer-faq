### Do I need to upkeep any debuffs?
Yes. **Demon Cry** and **Demon chain** inflict a debuff on the boss which debuffs its PDR and accuracy. The accuracy debuff triggers our passive **Insult to Injury** which grants 15% final damage so its critical to maintain a debuff on the boss at all times.

### How do I DPS on this class?
During burst (while **Demon Awakening** is active) your lashes become empowered, where you want to use your basic attack key to consume your lash stacks. Because lash stacks are capped, you want to maximize the buffs utilized while lashing. This means your burst will look something like:

Sequence -> Bane -> Lash until your special skill ring expires -> Ring swap key -> Demon Impact until your next special ring is up -> Activate Ring -> Spend the rest of your lash stacks

It's important to note you should be using demon chain off cooldown even during lash periods. After awakening ends, you should be using both demon chain and the empowered cerberus chomp (not the regular version) off cooldown.

### Chaos Lock Techniques
Chaos Lock has a couple of factors to the skill itself, including the direction that the character is facing, and holding arrow keys as you cast the skill.
- **Arrow Keys**: Using Chaos Lock and any arrow keys together (up to two keys at once) will force your character in the direction of the arrows that you are pressing.
- **Targets**: The target of Chaos Lock must be on sufficient ground for you to be able to teleport it, trying to teleport to flying mobs or mobs that are at the edge of a map will not work.
- **Range**: Chaos Lock has a set range and it will not be able to "detect" mobs beyond that range, thus making you unable to teleport to monsters beyond that range.
- **Combat Stance**: Chaos lock has the ability to put your character in combat stance without procing special nodes such as Fatal Strike, as long as there is no target in sight.
- **Character Direction**: The direction that your character is facing has an effect on where you'll end up teleporting while using chaos lock.
Face the boss - and you'll teleport to his front, as such, if you Face AWAY from the boss - you'll teleport to his back, as shown in the GIFs:

![Chaos Lock Backwards](chaoslock-backwards-text.gif)

Utilizing this feature, you can avoid certain boss mechanics. For example its very convenient to TP across purple meteors in Black Mage, fire walls in Seren or the tri colored lasers in Adversary P3.

### How do I burst on this class?

There are generally pretty big burst differences between if you have a RoR4 or a RoR5. This is because of the backloaded nature of our origin.

For RoR4 users, you will use the standard buff sequence without ring activation in it. For origin burst your rotation would look like:

**Nightmare** -> **Sequence** -> **Snake** + **RoR** at 15s left on origin -> **Bane** -> End **Nightmare** right before it expires.

If the boss is at risk of disappearing after the bind ends, you can instead use **Snake** + **RoR** at 5s left on the origin bind timer and end **Nightmare** 2s before bind ends. For non origin bursts, you would simply use your normal burst sequence followed by **Snake** + **RoR** whenever they come off cooldown.

With a RoR5, the duration becomes long enough so that you can add ring activation to your sequence. This simplifies your origin burst to just:

**Nightmare** -> **Snake** -> **Sequence** -> **Bane** -> End **Nightmare** right before it expires.

While for non origin bursts you simply follow the same sequence but without origin.

Note that without a cooldown hat, using sequence after origin makes you at risk of desyncing in party play. This is one of the reasons why cooldown hat is considered BiS. To avoid desyncing without a cooldown hat, you can use your sequence prior to origin (but you will need to remove the ring activation from it). Then use **Snake** + **RoR** after origin, either immediately if you have a RoR5 or at 15s left on origin if you have a RoR4. This is a slightly lower damage rotation but avoids desync.

### Culvert Rotation

## 1. Setup & Pre-Entry
*   **Auto Buff**: Ensure `Weapon Aura` is set to auto-buff.
*   **Sequence**: Prepare your buff sequence:
    `Boundless Rage` → `Demonic Fortitude` → `Mastema` → `Impenetrable Skin` → `Demon Awakening` → `Orthrus` → `Otherworld's Blessing`
*   **Gene Iframe**: Pre-pop Genesis Iframe shortly before entering.
*   **Timing**: Enter Culvert at **42 seconds** remaining on **Demon Awakening** (or **43s** if using a CD hat).

## 2. Entry & Early Phase
*   **Fatal Strike Proc**: Explode Genesis Iframe shortly after entering to proc `Fatal Strike`. If your not liberated, you can just proc it using any attack, but beware this makes it difficult to swap your hat if your doing so.
*   **Spirit of Rage**: Only pre-plant if you have `-4 Hat + TOF`. Otherwise, use it later.
*   **Gear Swaps**: 
    1. Swap off `TOF Cape`. 
    2. Swap off `CD Hat` unless it has extremely high stats.
*   **Phasing**: Use `Infernal Concussion` to phase until you can no longer one-shot mobs. Remember to debuff all phases you dont insta kill.

## 3. Entry Burst and DPM
*   **Demon Bane Timing**: 
    *   If using a CD hat: Start `Demon Bane` at **(122 - CDR)** seconds (e.g., 120s for -2, 118s for -4).
    *   *Note: Add 1s if you do not have 250 Mercedes Legion.*
*   **AB Link**: Pop `Angelic Buster Link` once you stop one-shotting stages.
*   **First Awakening**: Transition to lashing and use `Demon Chain` off-cooldown.
*   **Post-Awakening**: Use demon impact, while weaving in empowered chomps when they come off cooldown.
*   **Ascent Casts**: Use ascent casts while your continuous ring is on cooldown. Be careful to only cast it when it wouldn't delay a fatal proc. If you need to skip a cont downtime or not depends on your CD hat and how fast you phase so its difficult to give a general answer.

## 4. End Burst
*   **Will/Seren Nodes**: Pop at **53s**.
*   **Ring Swap**: Swap `Cont -> ROR` at a time where it will be up when you want to cast it. For example with a RoR5 and -4 hat this would be at around 29s, while with a RoR4 and no CD hat it would be right before you cast origin. Don't bother using preset swap to swap to a CD hat, it's better to just remain in CD hat.
*   **Origin**: Use `Nightmare` at **25s** left.
*   **The Finish**:
    *   Pop `ROR` and `AB Link` based on your specific durations so they last until the end.
    *   **Spirit of Rage**: Use `Spirit of Rage` at **16s** left. If its not up in time because you casted it at the start, you need to cast it sooner at the start (or not at all if you lack enough CDR).
    *   **Demon Bane**: Pop when off cooldown (if used early) or after a `Demonic Spear` fires.
    *   **Origin**: End `Nightmare` at **2-3s** left, aligning with your fatal strike.
